Coxinha

Description

Coxinha is a classic Brazilian snack with a crispy golden coating and a savory shredded chicken filling. This easy recipe delivers authentic flavor with simple ingredients, perfect for snacks, parties, or appetizers.

Ingredients

    Dough
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt
    • Filling
  • 1½ cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt & pepper
    • For coating and frying
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Make the filling: Sauté onion in oil, add chicken, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside.
  2. Prepare the dough: Bring broth, butter, and salt to a boil. Add flour all at once, stirring until it forms a smooth dough. Let cool slightly.
  3. Shape: Take a small piece of dough, flatten it, add filling, and shape into a teardrop.
  4. Coat: Dip in egg, then roll in breadcrumbs.
  5. Fry: Fry in hot oil until golden brown. Drain and serve hot.
