Caipirinha

Home

Description

A refreshing Brazilian classic, the Caipirinha blends fresh lime, sugar and cachaça into a bright cocktail, that's simple and made for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 big green lime
  • 2 ts of sugar
  • cachaça (enought to fill the cup)
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. Take the lemon, place it horizontally and cut off both ends, then turn it vertically and cut it in half, remove the middle (white part) of the lemon and slice it.
  2. Place the sliced ​​lemon and two heaping tablespoons of sugar in a glass suitable for the drink and, using a muddler, press until all the juice is extracted from the lemon.
  3. Fill the glass almost to the top with ice cubes (approximately 12 small ice cubes) and then fill the glass with cachaça.
  4. Stir well with a spoon or put it in a cocktail shaker and serve!
